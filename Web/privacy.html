Your Privacy & Security Matters

At FastPast, we are committed to protecting your data and ensuring a secure downloading experience with industry-leading privacy standards.

SSL Encrypted Zero Tracking Anonymous No Data Storage

Data Encryption & Protection

We employ industry-leading encryption methods to safeguard your data during transmission and storage. Our comprehensive security measures include:

  • SSL/TLS Encryption: All data transferred between your device and our servers is encrypted using secure socket layer (SSL) or transport layer security (TLS) protocols with 256-bit encryption.
  • Secure Servers: Our servers are hosted in secure data centers with robust physical and digital security protocols, including 24/7 monitoring and biometric access controls.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments to identify and address potential risks, ensuring continuous protection.

Enhanced Security Features

Beyond our core privacy commitments, FastPast offers advanced security features to give you peace of mind and ultimate protection:

  • Client-Side Virus Scan: Before any download starts, we integrate with the VirusTotal API to provide a client-side virus scan badge, ensuring your files are completely safe.
  • Zero-Knowledge Encrypted Vault: Store your sensitive downloads in a secure, zero-knowledge encrypted "vault" folder, protected by a second password with AES-256 encryption.
  • Onion-Service Mirror: For ultimate anonymity, access FastPast through our dedicated Onion-service mirror with a QR code for Tor users, ensuring complete privacy.

Data Retention Policy

We believe in minimal data retention and maximum privacy protection. Here's what you need to know:

  • No Personal Data Storage: We do not store any personally identifiable information related to your downloads. Your privacy is our priority.
  • Temporary Download Logs: Non-identifiable logs of download requests are kept for a very limited period (maximum 24 hours) for operational purposes, such as troubleshooting and abuse prevention, and are then permanently deleted.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We never share, sell, or rent your data to third parties. Your information stays with us and is never monetized.

User Control Over Data

You have complete control over your data when using FastPast. We empower users with full privacy autonomy:

  • Anonymous Usage: Our service is designed for anonymous use, meaning you don't need an account to download. No registration, no tracking, no hassle.
  • No Tracking: We do not use persistent cookies or tracking technologies to monitor your individual downloading activity. Your browsing remains private.
  • Data Deletion: Since we don't store personal download history, there's no personal data for you to request deletion of. Privacy by design.

Transparency & Reports

We are committed to transparency regarding our data practices and believe in open communication with our users.

While we do not currently publish formal transparency reports due to our minimal data collection approach, we are always open to inquiries about our security measures and privacy practices. For any questions regarding our privacy and security protocols, please don't hesitate to check our FAQ section or contact our support team.
