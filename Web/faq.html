Video Format Options: FastPast supports multiple video formats to ensure compatibility with your devices and needs:

MP4 (Recommended): Universal format compatible with virtually every deviceâ€”phones, tablets, computers, smart TVs. MP4 is the best choice for most users due to its excellent quality-to-file-size ratio and universal support.
